Welcome to Sharpen Reading for Schools!

Using our evidence-based, highly effective curriculum your students can improve a grade-level in reading in as little as 3 months. Our integrated progress monitoring personalizes the learning experience to adjust to each student's development.

With over 100 hours of learning content and 35+ digital activities, within a structured program, your students will be motivated to build their core reading skills & confidence at every step of their reading journeys.

Sharpen Reading focuses on:

  • Systematic and explicit instruction in phonics
  • Advanced phonemic awareness
  • Decoding and fluency
  • Handwriting and spelling practice
