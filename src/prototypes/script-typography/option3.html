Here's how you write /s/.

You are referring to the letter SOUND.

Press the "Animate" button and then recite the instructions for forming the letter in sync with the animation.

Say

Slide across.

Swing around.

Repeat the animation while reciting the instructions for forming the letter.

Say

Now it's your turn to write /s/.

Put your finger or cursor on the dot.

Help your Learner put her finger on the dot on the model in the handwriting box.

Repeat the directions for forming the letter.

Note

Your Learner should perform each step as you say it.

After your Learner has formed the entire letter, have her say its sound.

Repeat these steps until your Learner has written the letter a total of 4 times.
