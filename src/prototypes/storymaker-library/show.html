Frog & Log

Built by Jurickson

mail

A green frog sat on a log. The frog's name was Hop. Hop lived in a small town near a pond.

One day Hop met a red frog. The red frog said, 'Hi, I'm Ribbit.' Hop and Ribbit sat on the log.

Hop and Ribbit had fun all day. They jumped and played in the pond. Hop said, 'You are my best friend.'
