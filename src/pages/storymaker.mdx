## Character Choice

This is our first child screen. Because page instructions and interface
choices will often contain language that is beyond the child's reading level,
the interface will speak these out loud.

When the interface reads choice options aloud, it should be coordinated
with animation that displays/highlights the option as it is spoken, to help
the child connect what they hear to what they see on the page.
